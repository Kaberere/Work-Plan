TOP MANAGEMENT/ DIRECTORS/ DEPUTY DIRECTORS (KSG 2-3)


Please note:

  • Sum of Scores = Summation of Scores given across each of the values and competencies
  • 60 = maximum scores in this section i.e (5 x 12)

Strategic Visioning: Anticipates possible future events and developments. A catalyst for change. Translator of vision into action. Empowers employees to achieve desired performance. Leads others in strategy formulation and execution.


Change Leadership: Identifies windows for opportunity and takes advantage .Responds promptly to unforeseen changes. Question conventional approaches. Proactively identifies new initiatives for change management.


Mentoring: Delegates responsibility; gives staff autonomy in performing tasks. Values all staff input and expertise. Appreciates and rewards achievement and effort. Involves others while making decisions that affect them. Encourages staff to set challenging goals.


Decision Making: Makes well informed; effective and timely decisions; Perceives impacts and implication of decisions; Takes responsibility for actions and decisions made; Analyses situations, diagnose problems and identifies the key issues; Evaluates alternative course of action and produces a logical, practical and acceptable solution.


Collaborations and Partnerships: Ability to develop relationships with partners and stakeholders; Creates forum to share policy outcome; Lobbies partners and stakeholders to achieve outcomes; Influences partners and stakeholders; Establishes and maintains linkages.


Protocol and Diplomacy: Ability to uphold the etiquette and courtesy rules of diplomacy and affairs of office; ability to build coalitions internally and with other government agencies, international organizations, non -profit and private sector organizations to achieve common goals; Conducts negotiations and resolves issues through peaceful means Seeks to understand colleagues and what motivates.
