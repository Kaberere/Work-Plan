HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS/ MANAGERS (KSG 4-6)


Please note:

  • Sum of Scores = Summation of Scores given across each of the values and competencies
  • 60 = maximum scores in this section i.e (5 x 12)

Risk Management: Mitigate uncertainty in decision making; do environmental scanning and recommend best alternatives; minimize possibility of loss due to occurrence of risk; seek alternative solutions; generate ideas to facilitate prudent management action. (preventative measures, monitoring of risk)


Resources Management: Manage financial and non-financial resources efficiently and effectively; guard against prejudice to the financial interest of the School; adhere to applicable procedures in acquisition and disposal of assets; oversee work processes to achieve quality results within budgetary provisions; adopt accountability measures for prudent use of resources


Knowledge Management: Apply broad knowledge of theory and principles within their profession to solve managerial challenges; collate, analyze and derive information from data to inform decision making; nurture talent; facilitate succession management; promote knowledge sharing in the department.


Managing Performance: Interprets and implements strategic plans; promotes culture of performance; delegates effectively; monitor progress against milestones and deadlines; consistently delivers results.


Coaching: Clearly communicates departmental objectives, targets and performance indicators; monitors and tracks team performance; provide clear and constructive feedback; inspires team members to perform at their best; facilitates development of desired competencies and values.
